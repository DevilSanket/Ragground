## Caption

Scrambled eggs… but make them a little more interesting.
Next time you’re making eggs, swap out your usual butter or oil for miso butter.
Same pan. Same method. Very different result.

How to:
- Heat your pan and add a spoon of miso butter
- Crack in your eggs and scramble them the way you like -
soft curds, tight curds, runny, cooked through… your call
- Add salt, but lightly - miso carries its own salt
- Finish with a few torn basil leaves

That’s it.
It’s a small change, but it shifts the whole flavour of something you probably make all the time.
If you’ve got a jar of our miso chilli butter at home, try this tomorrow morning.
If you don’t… check the link in our bio.
