# Seaweed Miso Prawns

_A quick and flavorful recipe for sweet water prawns cooked in a hot cast iron pan with garlic, olive oil, and a rich seaweed miso butter, finished with fresh chives and lemon._

🍽️ Asian  |  📊 Easy

## Ingredients

- Fresh sweet water prawns
- Garlic
- Olive oil
- Salted butter
- Seaweed Miso
- Chives
- Lemon

## Ground Up Products Used

## Instructions

1. Prepare fresh sweet water prawns by removing the vein and splitting them open.
2. Heat a cast iron pan until very hot.
3. Slowly cook garlic in olive oil in the hot pan.
4. Mix salted butter with Seaweed Miso (2 parts miso : 1 part butter).
5. Cook the prawns in the pan, incorporating the seaweed miso butter.
6. Finish the dish with fresh chives and a squeeze of lemon.
