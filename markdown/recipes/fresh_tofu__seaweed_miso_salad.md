# Fresh Tofu & Seaweed Miso Salad

_A simple and fresh salad featuring custardy tofu, crisp vegetables, and a unique seaweed miso dressing, perfect for a light meal._

🕐 Lunch, snack  |  📊 Easy  |  ⏱️ 10 minutes

## Source

- **Instagram Reel:** [@Ground Up](https://www.instagram.com/reel/DY_sr7kKS0x/)

## Ingredients

- 2 tbsp Seaweed Miso
- 1 tsp fermented chilli (or fresh red chilli)
- 1 tsp grated ginger
- Black pepper (to taste)
- Lemon juice (from half a lemon, or to taste)
- A drizzle of roasted sesame oil
- A little honey
- A splash of Seaweed Tamari (or soy sauce)
- Tomato wedges
- Cucumber
- White onion
- Fresh, custard-y tofu
- Basil (for garnish)
- Scallions (for garnish)

## Instructions

1. Combine Seaweed Miso, fermented chilli, grated ginger, black pepper, lemon juice, sesame oil, honey, and Seaweed Tamari in a bowl to create the dressing.
2. Mix the dressing well, taste, and adjust seasonings as needed until you love it.
3. In a separate bowl, combine the tomato wedges, cucumber, white onion, and fresh tofu.
4. Pour the prepared dressing over the vegetables and tofu.
5. Toss everything together gently until all ingredients are well coated with the dressing.
6. Finish by garnishing the salad with fresh basil and scallions.
