# Elevated Instant Noodles with Toasted Sesame Miso

_Transform a simple instant noodle cup into a flavorful, umami-rich midnight snack in just 4 minutes by adding Ground Up's Toasted Sesame Miso._

🍽️ Asian  |  🕐 Snack  |  📊 Easy  |  ⏱️ 4 minutes

## Source

- **Instagram Reel:** [@Ground Up](https://www.instagram.com/reel/DNGLlj_KCON/)
- **Date:** 2025-08-08  |  **Likes:** 370

## Ingredients

- Instant noodles packet (including noodles and seasoning)
- Ground Up Toasted Sesame Miso
- Scallion (optional)
- Hot water

## Ground Up Products Used

- **Toasted Sesame Miso** — [shop via link in bio](https://www.instagram.com/reel/DNGLlj_KCON/)

## Instructions

1. Open your instant noodles packet.
2. (Optional) Add some or all of the instant noodle seasoning packet.
3. Add a bit of Ground Up Toasted Sesame Miso to the mix.
4. (Optional) Add scallion.
5. Pour in hot water.
6. Poke the noodles and miso to ensure the miso penetrates and mixes well.
7. Wait for the noodles to cook (approximately 4 minutes).
