# Sweet Water Prawns with Seaweed Miso

_A simple and flavorful recipe for sweet water prawns, cooked quickly in a hot pan with garlic, olive oil, and a special blend of salted butter and Ground Up's Seaweed Miso, finished with chives and lemon._

🍽️ Asian-inspired  |  🕐 Dinner  |  📊 Easy

## Ingredients

- fresh sweet water prawns
- garlic
- olive oil
- salted butter
- Seaweed Miso
- chives
- lemon

## Instructions

1. Prepare prawns by removing veins and splitting them open.
2. Heat a very hot pan, preferably cast iron.
3. Slowly cook garlic in olive oil.
4. Mix salted butter with Seaweed Miso (2 parts miso : 1 part butter).
5. Add prawns to the hot pan and cook with the garlic and miso butter mixture.
6. Finish with chives and lemon at the end to balance the flavors.
