# Miso Tomato Pasta Sauce

_A simple and quick pasta dish featuring a rich tomato sauce enhanced with Ground Up's white miso for added depth and umami, offering a familiar yet distinct flavor._

🍽️ Fusion  |  🕐 Lunch | dinner  |  📊 Easy  |  ⏱️ 10 minutes

## Ingredients

- 3 tomatoes
- garlic
- olive oil
- Ground Up white miso
- pepper
- fresh basil
- pasta
- salt
- sugar

## Ground Up Products Used

- **white miso** — [shop via link in bio](https://www.instagram.com/reel/DO6BMtzihj1/)

## Instructions

1. Blanch 3 tomatoes until the skin easily peels off, then crush them by hand.
2. Sauté garlic gently in olive oil.
3. Add crushed tomatoes and slow simmer until the raw edge is gone.
4. Stir in Ground Up white miso.
5. Season with sugar, salt, and pepper.
6. Finish with torn basil.
7. Toss with cooked pasta.
8. Drizzle with olive oil.
