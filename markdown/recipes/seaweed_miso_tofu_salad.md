## Instructions

1. Prepare the dressing by combining 2 tbsp Seaweed Miso, 1 tsp fermented chilli (or fresh red chilli), 1 tsp grated ginger, black pepper, lemon juice, a drizzle of sesame oil, a little honey, and a splash of Seaweed Tamari (or soy sauce).
2. Mix the dressing well, taste, and adjust seasonings as needed.
3. In a bowl, combine tomato wedges, cucumber, white onion, and fresh, custard-y tofu.
4. Pour the prepared dressing over the vegetables and tofu.
5. Toss gently to ensure all ingredients are coated.
6. Finish by garnishing with fresh basil and scallions.
7. Adjust with additional lemon juice, salt, and pepper to taste.
