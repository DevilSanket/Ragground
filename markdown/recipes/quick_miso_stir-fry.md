# Quick Miso Stir-Fry

_A simple and flavorful stir-fry recipe featuring Ground Up's classic soybean miso, perfect for a quick weekday meal that tastes like you put in more effort._

## Ingredients

- chopped red chilli (or green)
- toasted sesame oil
- raw sugar
- soy sauce
- rice vinegar
- grated ginger
- Ground Up classic Soybean Miso
- chicken (or other protein)
- broccoli (or other veggies)
- onions
- hot rice (for serving)

## Instructions

1. Mix chopped red or green chilli, toasted sesame oil, raw sugar, soy sauce, rice vinegar, grated ginger, and Ground Up classic Soybean Miso to create the stir-fry sauce.
2. Toss in chicken, broccoli, and onion (or your choice of protein and vegetables).
3. Add the prepared stir-fry sauce to the pan and give it a good stir on high heat, allowing it to sizzle until everything is cooked and combined.
4. Serve hot, preferably with rice, and enjoy your meal.
