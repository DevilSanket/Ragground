# Garlic Miso Prawns

_A simple and quick recipe for buttery, garlicky prawns marinated in a miso blend and strawberry vinegar, cooked in under 30 minutes for a delicious, lip-smacking meal._

🕐 Dinner  |  📊 Easy  |  ⏱️ 30 minutes

## Ingredients

- prawns
- garlic
- red chilli
- white pepper
- Ground Up strawberry vinegar
- Ground Up garlic miso blend
- butter
- basil
- salt

## Instructions

1. Slice garlic and red chilli.
2. Marinate prawns in garlic miso blend, sliced garlic, red chilli, white pepper, and strawberry vinegar for 15-20 minutes.
3. Heat butter in a pan until it starts to bubble.
4. Sizzle sliced garlic in the butter.
5. Drop in the marinated prawns, cook for one minute on one side, then flip.
6. Add basil and optionally some salt.
