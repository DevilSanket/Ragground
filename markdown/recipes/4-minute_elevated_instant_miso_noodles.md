# 4-Minute Elevated Instant Miso Noodles

_Learn how to transform instant noodles into a gourmet 3 AM snack in just 4 minutes, featuring Ground Up's Toasted Sesame Miso for an umami-packed flavor._

🍽️ Asian  |  🕐 Snack  |  📊 Easy  |  ⏱️ 4 minutes

## Source

- **Instagram Reel:** [@Ground Up](https://www.instagram.com/reel/DNGLlj_KCON/)

## Ingredients

- instant noodles
- instant noodle seasoning/cheesy powder (optional)
- Ground Up Toasted Sesame Miso
- scallions
- hot water

## Ground Up Products Used

- **Toasted Sesame Miso** — [shop via link in bio](https://www.instagram.com/reel/DNGLlj_KCON/)

## Instructions

1. Prepare instant noodles (add seasoning packet, optionally reduce cheesy powder).
2. Add a generous amount of Ground Up Toasted Sesame Miso and chopped scallions to the cup.
3. Pour in hot water.
4. Gently poke and stir to ensure the miso penetrates and dissolves.
5. Enjoy the soupy, nutty, and umami-rich noodles.
