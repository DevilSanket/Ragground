# Miso Tomato Sauce Pasta

_A quick and easy 10-minute meal, this miso tomato sauce pasta offers classic comfort with a deeper, richer, and umami-packed flavor profile._

## Ingredients

- 3 tomatoes
- Garlic
- Olive oil
- White miso
- Sugar
- Salt
- Pepper
- Fresh basil
- Pasta

## Instructions

1. Blanch and peel 3 tomatoes until the skin easily peels off, then crush them by hand.
2. Gently sizzle garlic in olive oil.
3. Add the crushed tomatoes and let them simmer slowly until the raw edge is gone.
4. Stir in white miso, then season with sugar, salt, and pepper.
5. Finish with torn fresh basil and toss with cooked pasta.
