# Miso Butter Scrambled Eggs

_A quick and easy recipe to elevate classic scrambled eggs by swapping regular butter for Ground Up's miso butter, adding a rich umami flavor._

🕐 Breakfast  |  📊 Easy

## Ingredients

- eggs
- miso butter
- salt
- basil leaves

## Ground Up Products Used

## Instructions

1. Heat your pan and add a spoon of miso butter
2. Crack in your eggs and scramble them the way you like - soft curds, tight curds, runny, cooked through… your call
3. Add salt, but lightly - miso carries its own salt
4. Finish with a few torn basil leaves
