# Scrambled Eggs with Miso Butter

_Elevate your everyday scrambled eggs by swapping out regular butter or oil for a flavorful miso butter, creating a unique and savory twist._

🕐 Breakfast  |  📊 Easy

## Source

- **Instagram Reel:** [@Unknown](https://www.instagram.com/p/DU-2EP4Cvym/)
- **Date:** 2026-06-08  |  **Likes:** N/A

## Ingredients

- miso butter
- eggs
- salt
- torn basil leaves

## Ground Up Products Used

- **miso chilli butter** — [shop via link in bio](https://www.instagram.com/p/DU-2EP4Cvym/)

## Instructions

1. Heat your pan and add a spoon of miso butter.
2. Crack in your eggs and scramble them to your preferred consistency (soft curds, tight curds, runny, or cooked through).
3. Add salt lightly, as miso already contains salt.
4. Finish with a few torn basil leaves.
