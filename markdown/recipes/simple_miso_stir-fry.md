# Simple Miso Stir-fry

_A quick and easy stir-fry recipe featuring a homemade miso sauce, perfect for a simple weekday meal._

🍽️ Asian  |  🕐 Lunch/dinner  |  📊 Easy

## Ingredients

- Chopped red or green chilli
- Toasted sesame oil
- Raw sugar
- Soy sauce
- Rice vinegar
- Grated ginger
- Ground Up Classic Soybean Miso
- Chicken
- Broccoli
- Onion
- Hot rice (for serving)

## Ground Up Products Used

- **Ground Up Classic Soybean Miso** — [shop via link in bio](https://www.instagram.com/reel/DM0Kq2EP_ec/)

## Instructions

1. Prepare the stir-fry sauce by mixing chopped red or green chilli, toasted sesame oil, raw sugar, soy sauce, rice vinegar, grated ginger, and Ground Up Classic Soybean Miso.
2. Toss in chicken, broccoli, and onion (or other preferred protein and vegetables).
3. Add the prepared stir-fry sauce to the chicken and vegetables.
4. Give everything a good stir on high heat, allowing it to sizzle until cooked through and combined.
5. Serve hot, preferably with rice, and enjoy.
