# Quick Miso Butter Prawns

_A quick and easy recipe for succulent prawns marinated in a garlic miso blend, then pan-fried in butter with basil._

🕐 Dinner/snack  |  📊 Easy  |  ⏱️ 30 minutes

## Ingredients

- Prawns
- Garlic
- Red Chilli
- White Pepper
- Ground Up Strawberry Vinegar
- Ground Up Garlic Miso Blend
- Butter
- Basil
- Salt (optional)

## Instructions

1. Slice garlic and red chilli.
2. Marinate prawns with sliced garlic, sliced red chilli, white pepper, Ground Up strawberry vinegar, and Ground Up garlic miso blend.
3. Let the prawns marinate for 15-20 minutes.
4. Heat butter in a pan until it bubbles, then add the remaining sliced garlic and let it sizzle.
5. Add the marinated prawns to the pan, cook for one minute on one side.
6. Flip the prawns and cook for another minute.
7. Add basil and optional salt.
