## Original Caption

Our fresh tofu meets Seaweed Miso.

For the dressing:
— 2 tbsp Seaweed Miso
— 1 tsp fermented chilli (or fresh red chilli)
— 1 tsp grated ginger
— Black pepper
— Lemon juice
— A drizzle of sesame oil
— A little honey
— A splash of Seaweed Tamari (or soy sauce)

Mix, taste, adjust. Make sure you love the dressing before it meets the vegetables.

Then toss with:
— Tomato wedges
— Cucumber
— White onion (very much in season right now)
— Our fresh, custard-y tofu

Finish with basil and scallions.
