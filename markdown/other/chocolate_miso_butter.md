## Original Transcript

It's been raining a lot lately and this kind of weather makes me crave the simple cosy things. Like this chocolate miso butter. All you need is unsalted precedent butter, cocoa powder, we get asked from Tanshikar farms, some caster sugar and black bean miso because obviously they will always be miso. Start by whisking softened butter which is at room temperature until it's nice and fluffy. Add the caster sugar and keep whisking till it dissolves. Sift in your cocoa powder and whisk it till it all comes together and then fold in the miso. That's it! Savory, sweet, fruity from the miso. A little unexpected, just like this weather.
