## Original Caption

Pumpkin & Miso Soup
~ 2-3 servings

Char pumpkin (about 250 gms, no need to peel) on a cast iron tawa or pan with a slick of olive oil. 
Separately fry 5-6 crushed garlic pods, 2 chillies & a handful of herbs (rosemary, marjoram, sage) in EVOO. Add the charred pumpkin along with veg stock or water + stock cube (a pinch of mushroom granules from urban platter works a treat). The stock should cover the pumpkin fully. Let it all cook and soften for about 20 mins. 

Blend the soup along with a big spoonful or two of confit garlic miso from @groundup.in. Adjust consistency & seasoning right in the end. Serve with a dollop of sour cream/ chunks of feta, toasted seeds/ nuts (we have a lovely Savoury Granola that's perfect for topping this) & EVOO fried sourdough bread for dipping. Fin.

P.S: Add a drizzle or two of @groundup.in Chilli oil for extra heat.
