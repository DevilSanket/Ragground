## Caption

Classic comfort, with miso quietly doing the heavy lifting.
Our miso tomato sauce takes what you know and makes it deeper, richer, and full of umami.

Here's how to make it :

 – Boil & peel 3 tomatoes, crush by hand 
– Sauté garlic in olive oil, add tomatoes, let it simmer 
– Stir in white miso, season with sugar, salt, pepper 
– Finish with torn basil & toss with pasta 

Drizzle of olive oil. Fresh basil. Done. 
Because there will always be miso.
