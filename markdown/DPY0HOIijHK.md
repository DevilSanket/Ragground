## Caption

A grilled cheese — but with miso quietly doing its thing.
Sweet onions, melted cheese, and that deep umami from our classic soybean miso.

Here’s how to make it:

-Mix sliced onions with miso
-Sauté till soft and caramelized
-Fold with mozzarella and cheddar
-Layer between slices of bread (We used one of the best sourdough breads in town - courtesy of @thegoodbutter.in )
-Grill till golden and finish with a drizzle of honey

Crisp edges. Gooey cheese.
And as always - there will be miso!
