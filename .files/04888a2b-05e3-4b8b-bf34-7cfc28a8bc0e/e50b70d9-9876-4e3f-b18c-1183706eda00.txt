Title: Miso Tomato Pasta Sauce

Summary: This reel provides a step-by-step guide to making a quick and delicious miso tomato pasta sauce. It highlights how Ground Up's white miso enhances a classic comfort dish with depth and umami, making it a simple yet flavorful meal.

Ingredients:
- 3 tomatoes
- garlic
- olive oil
- Ground Up white miso
- pepper
- fresh basil
- pasta
- salt
- sugar

Instructions:
1. Blanch 3 tomatoes until the skin easily peels off, then crush them by hand.
2. Sauté garlic gently in olive oil.
3. Add crushed tomatoes and slow simmer until the raw edge is gone.
4. Stir in Ground Up white miso.
5. Season with sugar, salt, and pepper.
6. Finish with torn basil.
7. Toss with cooked pasta.
8. Drizzle with olive oil.
