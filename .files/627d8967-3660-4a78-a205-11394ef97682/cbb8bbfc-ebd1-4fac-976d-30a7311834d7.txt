Title: Ground Up Cheat Code Noodle Soup

Summary: This reel demonstrates how to quickly prepare a delicious and satisfying noodle soup using a homemade broth base, noodles, vegetables, and Ground Up miso. It highlights the ease of preparation and the rich flavors achieved with the brand's 'cheat code' product.

Ingredients:
- Ground Up cheat code (miso/broth base)
- noodles
- vegetables
- hot water
- rice vinegar
- chili oil

Instructions:
1. Prepare the Ground Up cheat code/broth base.
2. Pour hot water over the base.
3. Add noodles and vegetables.
4. Cook the noodles.
5. Add rice vinegar for balance.
6. Mix well to combine all flavors, including chili oil.
7. Serve and enjoy.
