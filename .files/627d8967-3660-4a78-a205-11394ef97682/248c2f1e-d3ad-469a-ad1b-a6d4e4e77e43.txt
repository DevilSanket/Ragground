[recipe] Quick Miso Stir-Fry

Stir fries are supposed to be simple, flavourful yet taste like you tried harder. And our miso... just makes it better. 

Perfect for one of those weekday meals you don’t want to overthink.

Here's how you can make it:

-Mix chopped red or green chilli, toasted sesame oil, raw sugar, soy sauce, rice vinegar, grated ginger and Ground Up classic Soybean Miso
-Toss in chicken, broccoli and onion
-Give good stir on high heat and let it sizzle
-Serve hot, preferably with rice and enjoy!
