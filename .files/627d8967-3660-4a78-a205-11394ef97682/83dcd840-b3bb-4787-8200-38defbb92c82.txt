This Christmas, we're keeping it simple. Good food, meant to be shared.
A menu built around what we love to do: roasting, fermenting, and bringing flavors together that feel both familiar and a little unexpected.

Whey miso butter–roasted cauliflower, two-day-aged chicken with dark miso butter, and fermented chilli-glazed ham. All anchored by whipped mashed potatoes with nutmeg, flaky buttermilk biscuits, and a rich, peppery bone or mushroom gravy.

To balance it out, a bright salad with peas, beans, herbs, house made paneer shavings and wild pickled fern dressing, plus marinated tomatoes finished with tomato tamari.

And to finish, rustic Victoria sponge with roselle strawberry vinegar jelly, chocolate miso butter, and mulberry compote.

Reserve your seat for Christmas, the Ground Up way.
