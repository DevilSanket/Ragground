Chef @gdesai89 's go-to chocolate spread. (Thanks to @the_prabhu 's mind!)
Of course it has miso in it.
Quick, simple, and you can probably make it right now—
as long as there’s Black Bean Miso in your fridge.
And if you don’t, you can find it through the link in bio.

Here’s the recipe:
– Whisk unsalted butter until smooth 
– Add in sugar and continue whisking until it dissolves.
– Sift in cocoa powder and whisk
– Finish it off with a spoonful of black bean miso
